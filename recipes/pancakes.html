Pancake

Description

Pancakes are easy to make and are a great way to start your day.

You can flavour and decorate them with your favourite toppings.

Ingredients

  • 500 g self-raising flour
  • 200 ml sparkling water
  • 1 egg
  • pinch of salt
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp oil

Directions

  1. The easiest way is to place everything into an airtight container and shake well. You can also stir them in a normal container.
  2. Pour a small amount into a pan, flip when smells good.
