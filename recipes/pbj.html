PBJ

Description

There are mornings when you want to start your day with a simple breakfast.

Be aware though that creating a peanut butter and jelly sandwich is not easy. If you need help, you can use this instructional video. How to make a PB&J

Ingredients

  • 1 slice of bread
  • 1 jar of peanut butter
  • 1 bottle of jelly
  • 1 knife
  • 1 father with ASD

Directions

  1. Place the ingredients in front of the father.
  2. Ask the children to write instructions and ask the father to follow the instructions.
  3. Watch and have fun!
